Morning Breakfast

Pancakes

Recipe:
1. Mix flour, eggs & milk until smooth.
2. Heat a non-stick pan & melt butter.
3. Pour batter and cook both sides until golden.
4. Serve with syrup or fruit.

Scrambled Eggs

Recipe:
1. Crack eggs into bowl, whisk with milk.
2. Heat butter in pan.
3. Stir gently until soft folds form.
4. Season with salt & herbs.

Croissant

Recipe:
1. Prepare lamination dough.
2. Roll, fold, chill for layers.
3. Shape into croissants.
4. Proof & bake golden brown.

Toast

Recipe:
1. Butter bread slices.
2. Toast until golden.
3. Add jam or butter.
4. Serve hot.
