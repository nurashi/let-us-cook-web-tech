Morning Breakfast

Pancakes

Recipe:
1. Mix flour, eggs & milk until smooth.
2. Heat a non-stick pan & melt butter.
3. Pour batter and cook both sides until golden.
4. Serve with syrup or fruit.

Scrambled Eggs

Recipe:
1. Crack eggs into bowl, whisk with a splash of milk.
2. Heat butter in pan over medium-low heat.
3. Pour eggs in, stir gently until soft folds form.
4. Season with salt, pepper, herbs.

Croissant

Recipe:
1. Prepare lamination dough with butter layers.
2. Roll, fold, chill – repeat folds for flaky texture.
3. Cut into croissant shapes.
4. Proof & bake until golden brown.

Toast

Recipe:
1. Choose bread slices.
2. Butter one side lightly.
3. Brown in toaster or pan until crisp.
4. Spread jam or butter on top.

Waffle

Recipe:

Muffin

Recipe:
1. Mix dry ingredients – flour, sugar, baking powder.

Oatmeal

Recipe:
1. Boil water or milk.
2. Add oats, stir & simmer.
3. Cook until soft, stirring occasionally.
4. Add fruit, nuts or sweetener.
