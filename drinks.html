Refreshing Drinks

Cocktail

Recipe:
1. Mix juice, soda, or alcohol base.
2. Add ice cubes.
3. Stir or shake well.
4. Garnish with fruit slice.
Watch Recipe

Tea

Recipe:
1. Boil fresh water.
2. Add tea bag or loose tea.
3. Steep 3–5 minutes.
4. Add lemon or honey if desired.
Watch Recipe

Coffee

Recipe:
1. Grind coffee beans.
2. Brew with hot water.
3. Pour into a cup.
4. Add sugar or milk if you like.
Watch Recipe

Orange Juice

Recipe:
1. Cut oranges in half.
2. Squeeze with juicer.
3. Strain juice if needed.
4. Serve cold with ice.
Watch Recipe

Milk

Recipe:
1. Pour fresh milk into a glass.
2. Heat gently if warm milk preferred.
3. Optionally add honey or cocoa.
4. Enjoy immediately.
Watch Recipe

Water

Recipe:
1. Use clean filtered water.
2. Chill in fridge.
3. Pour into glass with ice.
4. Add lemon slice if desired.
Watch Recipe

Smoothie

Recipe:
1. Add fruits (banana, berries) to blender.
2. Add yogurt or milk.
3. Blend until creamy.
4. Serve chilled.
Watch Recipe

Cola

Recipe:
1. Chill bottle of cola.
2. Add ice cubes to a glass.
3. Pour cola over ice.
4. Serve cold.
Watch Recipe

Beer

Recipe:
1. Chill beer bottle.
2. Tilt glass at 45°.
3. Pour slowly to avoid foam.
4. Serve cold.
